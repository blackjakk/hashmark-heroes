Questions, answered

Is it really free?
Yes — the whole game. No account, no ads, no purchases. Open the page and play.
Do I need to install anything?
No. It's plain HTML/CSS/JavaScript running in your browser — nothing to download, nothing to update. If you can read this page, you can run the game.
Where do my saves live?
In your browser's local storage, with multiple franchise slots. Your league stays on your machine — just note that clearing site data clears saves, and saves don't follow you across devices.
Does it work on my phone?
Yes — the interface is responsive down to small phones. The broadcast is at its best on a bigger screen, but the full franchise runs anywhere your browser goes.
Do I have to call plays?
Only if you want to. Every game offers Call the Plays, Watch the broadcast with AI coaches, or Sim instantly — and you can hand a live game to Coach Mode mid-drive. Pure-GM careers are absolutely a thing.
How does multiplayer stay fair?
Every game is deterministic: one seed plus the two coaches' calls always re-simulates to the identical result, byte for byte. H2H matches produce a verifiable artifact — if a score were ever tampered with, a re-sim would prove it.
